where do lost things go

2D Motion Graphics

2023

Context

Where do everyday objects go when they vanish into nowhere? This short 2D animation explores a playful theory: lost items aren't missing, they've simply migrated inside the Earth. Driven by quirky storytelling and a bold illustration style, the short film visualizes this imagined theory, utilizing a looping narrative ending that connects back to the beginning.
