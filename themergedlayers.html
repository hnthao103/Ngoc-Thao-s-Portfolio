The Merged Layers

Experimental Model

2023

Context

This experimental model visualizes the steep wealth gap in Ho Chi Minh City across four distinct social layers. While the merged layers form a complete picture of the city, picking up the model triggers a physical mechanism where the inner layers slide out from underneath. This interactive separation reveals each societal class individually, symbolizing the dramatic transition between luxury and poverty in a coexisting urban environment.
