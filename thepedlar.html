The Pedlar

3D Modelling

2023

Context

The Pedlar is a steampunk robot deeply rooted in Vietnamese street culture. Reinterpreting traditional vendors who carry goods daily, the design captures how they spend hours resting on low stools. I integrated this iconic "Asian squat" posture directly into the robot’s mechanics and silhouette, blending cultural realism with mechanical fantasy.
